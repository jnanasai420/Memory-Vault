ABOUT US

De-clutter your drawers and store all your keepsakes in one convenient place.

HOW IT WORKS

Snap a photo of your keepsake for example, a birthday card, child's art project or a note from a loved one. Upload it to your Memory Vault App and select the category you want it to be stored in. Simply add the date and person to store it under so you can easily browse memories.

CUSTOMIZED CATEGORIES

Memory Vault is completely customizable allowing YOU to choose the way you store your memories. Create categories for your child's milestones such as first steps or even art projects. Don't bother searching through thousands of photos, everything is organized and at your fingertips.
